The Marvelous Koshary

First, let us indulge in the flavors of Koshary, a beloved Egyptian dish. This harmonious blend of rice, lentils, and pasta, topped with a tangy tomato sauce and crispy fried onions, is a true feast for the senses.

The Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Onions
  • Salt & pepper
  • chickpeas

How To Do It

  1. Boil the chickpeas with cumin while preparing the fried onions.
  2. Chop the onions into thin circles. Allow the onions tossed in flour to rest for 30 minutes prior to frying.
  3. Make the fresh red sauce. Add the bell pepper, tomatoes, onion, garlic cloves, ground cumin, ground coriander, salt, pepper, lime juice, and some oil from the fried onions in a food processor.
  4. Prepare the koshari lentil rice by blending the peeled onion in the food processor.
  5. Blend the tomatoes in a blender. Pour the pureed tomatoes into a strainer.
  6. Boil on medium-high heat for about 10 minutes. Add a chili pepper if desired.
  7. Assemble your koshari bowl!
